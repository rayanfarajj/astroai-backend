09 / Competitor Positioning
5 Ways to Stand Above the Competition
01

Lead with transformation, not features

Show before/after visuals first, explain property value and curb appeal gains second. Customers buy outcomes, not pressure PSI.

02

Own the recurring revenue angle

Position yearly or seasonal service plans as the default, not one-time jobs. Emphasize 15% savings and never-worry maintenance for loyal clients.

03

Localize all messaging

Always mention San Antonio, surrounding areas, and local social proof. Geo-targeted ads perform 3x better than regional messaging.

04

Make booking frictionless

Offer messenger chat, simple form, and phone booking equally. Reduce friction to appointment—fastest lead response wins the 90-day goal.

05

Use social proof early and often

Lead with review count and star rating in ads. Follow with customer testimonials and before/after galleries in nurture sequences. Trust is earned faster with proof.
