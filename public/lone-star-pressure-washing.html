08 / Lead Qualification Script
Ready-to-Use Call Script
Opening
Hi! Thanks for your interest in Lone Star Pressure Washing. I'm calling to see if we're a good fit for your property's cleaning needs.
Qualifying Questions
Q1
What type of property do you have—residential home, commercial, or both?
Why: Confirms property type and service eligibility.
Q2
What areas are you most concerned about—driveway, siding, roof, or something else?
Why: Identifies specific pain points and service scope.
Q3
Have you used pressure washing before, and if so, what was your experience?
Why: Gauges familiarity and potential concerns about quality.
Q4
Are you looking for a one-time cleaning or regular monthly maintenance?
Why: Determines if they're prospect for recurring contracts (key goal).
Q5
What's your timeline—do you need this done ASAP or are you flexible?
Why: Establishes urgency and appointment availability.
Transition to Close
Based on what you've shared, I think we can definitely help. Here's what makes us different: we specialize in recurring monthly service that keeps your property looking pristine without you lifting a finger.
Objection Handling
❌ "We can do this ourselves or hire cheaper competitors."
✅ I understand. Most DIY attempts cause damage costing thousands in repairs. Our $199/month recurring plan saves you time, prevents damage, and increases property value—it usually pays for itself.
❌ "I'm not sure about committing to a monthly service."
✅ No pressure at all! Most clients start with one cleaning to see our quality, then love the convenience of monthly maintenance. We can do a trial service with zero commitment.
❌ "I want to think about it or get other quotes."
✅ Absolutely—comparison shop! When you're ready, we offer a $50 discount for new clients booking this month. Fair enough?
