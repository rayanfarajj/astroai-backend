08 / Lead Qualification Script
Ready-to-Use Call Script
Opening
Hi [Name], thanks for your interest in Lone Star Pressure Washing! I want to make sure we're a good fit for what you need. Do you have 2 minutes?
Qualifying Questions
Q1
Are you looking to clean just the driveway, or other areas like siding and patio too?
Why: Understand scope and upsell opportunity for full exterior service.
Q2
When was the last time you had professional pressure washing done?
Why: Gauge urgency and determine if they're recurring-minded vs. one-time only.
Q3
Do you currently do your own maintenance or hire contractors for exterior work?
Why: Identify their preference for DIY vs. professional help.
Q4
Would you prefer a one-time service or recurring monthly/quarterly plan?
Why: Qualify for recurring revenue goal; non-recurring prospects may be low priority.
Q5
Is curb appeal and property maintenance important for resale value or just day-to-day satisfaction?
Why: Determine motivation and price sensitivity.
Transition to Close
Great! Based on what you've shared, I think we can definitely help. Let me schedule a free estimate so we can show you exactly what we can do.

❌ "Your price is higher than competitors."
✅ I hear that often. Our pricing reflects professional-grade equipment and reliability—we show up on schedule, every time. Plus, recurring plans get 20% off. Can I show you the math on annual savings?

✅ No problem! Our estimate takes 10 minutes, and you'll walk away knowing exactly what your property needs. Does Tuesday or Thursday work better this week?
09 / Competitor Positioning
5 Ways to Stand Above the Competition
01

Recurring Focus

Position Lone Star as the pressure washing company for recurring maintenance, not one-time cleanings. Emphasize 'set it and forget it' convenience and discounts for monthly contracts.

02

Local Expertise

Highlight knowledge of San Antonio climate, common algae/mold issues, and local property types. Build trust through location-specific before/afters and customer testimonials from recognizable neighborhoods.

03

Time Freedom

Lead with the benefit that busy homeowners get their property cleaned automatically without calling or scheduling. Emphasize peace of mind and curb appeal without effort.

04

Property Protection

Frame pressure washing as property investment protection that prevents costly damage and maintains resale value. Use health angle (algae/mold removal) to justify recurring investment.

05

Reliability

Position Lone Star as the professional alternative to DIY equipment and unreliable handymen. Guarantee show-ups and professional results every time to win recurring clients.
