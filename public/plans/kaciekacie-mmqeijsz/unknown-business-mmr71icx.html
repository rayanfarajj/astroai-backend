90-Day Roadmap
Week 1-2
Foundation & Setup

Install Meta Pixel and Conversions API on all landing pages. Build custom audiences and lookalike audiences from existing data. Create and A/B test landing pages with clear lead capture forms. Set up CRM integration for automated lead follow-up. Develop all ad creatives, copy variations, and UTM tracking parameters. Establish baseline KPIs and reporting dashboards.

Week 3-4
Launch & Test

Launch Pain Points and Empathy ad angles across Facebook and Instagram with $500 allocated to prospecting. Activate retargeting campaigns with $150 budget for warm audiences. Run 2-3 creative variations per ad angle for rapid testing. Monitor cost per lead, click-through rates, and landing page conversion rates daily. Begin qualification calls on all incoming leads within 24 hours.

Week 5-8
Optimize & Refine

Analyze performance data and pause underperforming ad sets (CPL above $40 or CTR below 1%). Introduce Proof and Curiosity ad angles to diversify the funnel. Shift budget toward top-performing audiences and creatives. Test new headlines, primary text, and image/video variations. Optimize landing page elements based on heatmap and conversion data. Refine retargeting sequences with updated messaging for non-converters.

Week 9-12
Scale & Expand

Increase budget allocation to top 3 performing ad sets by 20-30% incrementally. Expand lookalike audiences to 2-3% for broader reach while maintaining quality. Launch UGC-style video testimonials and case study creatives. Implement advanced retargeting sequences with multi-touch nurturing. Explore Advantage+ campaign formats for automated optimization. Compile full performance report with ROI analysis and recommendations for next quarter.
