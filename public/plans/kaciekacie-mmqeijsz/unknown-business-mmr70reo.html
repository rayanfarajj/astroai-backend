90-Day Roadmap
Week 1-2
Foundation & Setup

Install Meta Pixel and Conversions API, set up custom audiences and lookalikes, build landing pages with lead forms, configure CRM integration and lead notifications, create all ad creatives and copy variations across all five angles.

Week 3-4
Launch & Test

Launch Pain Points and Empathy ad angles with 3-4 audience segments each. Allocate 60% of budget to prospecting and 40% to testing. A/B test headlines, primary text, and creative formats (image vs. video vs. carousel). Activate retargeting campaigns for early website visitors and engagers.

Week 5-8
Optimize & Refine

Analyze performance data and pause underperforming ads and audiences. Double down on top 2-3 performing ad-audience combinations. Introduce Proof and Curiosity angles to combat ad fatigue. Optimize landing pages based on conversion rate data. Refine retargeting sequences with fresh creative and urgency messaging.

Week 9-12
Scale & Expand

Increase budget allocation to proven winners by 20-30% incrementally. Expand lookalike audiences to 2-3% for broader reach. Test new creative formats like testimonial videos and carousel case studies. Implement advanced retargeting with sequential messaging. Evaluate overall ROAS and CPL to set benchmarks for ongoing scaling beyond month three.
