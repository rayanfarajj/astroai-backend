90-Day Roadmap
Week 1
Foundation

Set up the Facebook and Instagram ad accounts, pixel tracking, lead forms, and creative assets to ensure every dollar is tracked from day one. Define baseline KPIs, establish the lead capture workflow, and confirm follow-up processes are in place before a single ad goes live.

Week 2
Launch

Deploy the first round of campaigns at $1000/day across Facebook and Instagram, testing at least two ad angles with multiple creatives to generate early data. Monitor delivery, click-through rates, and cost-per-lead closely to identify which audiences and messages are gaining the most traction.

Weeks 3-4
Optimize

Cut underperforming ad sets and reallocate budget toward the angles, creatives, and audiences showing the strongest lead quality and volume. A/B test headlines, primary text, and CTAs to incrementally improve click-through and conversion rates based on real campaign data.

Weeks 5-8
Scale

Scale winning ad sets with increased budgets while introducing new creative variations to prevent audience fatigue and maintain strong performance. Layer in retargeting campaigns to re-engage warm leads who clicked but did not convert, maximizing return on every dollar spent.

Weeks 9-12
Results

Consolidate the highest-performing campaigns and creatives into a repeatable system designed to consistently generate leads at a predictable cost. Deliver a full performance review against the 90-day goal of generating leads, with a forward-looking strategy for sustained growth beyond this quarter.
