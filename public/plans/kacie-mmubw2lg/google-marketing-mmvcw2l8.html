90-Day Roadmap
Week 1
Foundation

Build out the core campaign infrastructure including ad creatives, landing page or lead form, tracking pixels, and audience segments across Facebook and Instagram. Define the primary offer, set up the CRM or lead destination, and establish baseline KPIs to measure success from day one.

Week 2
Launch

Go live with the full $200 per day budget split across the Pain and Offer ad angles targeting cold audiences with the primary lead generation objective. Monitor early performance data daily and make micro-adjustments to targeting, placements, and creative to stabilize cost per lead.

Weeks 3-4
Optimize

Analyze which ad angles, headlines, and audiences are producing the lowest cost per lead and highest quality prospects, then pause underperformers and reallocate budget to winning combinations. Launch the retargeting campaign layer to re-engage warm audiences and increase overall conversion rate from click to booked call.

Weeks 5-8
Scale

Scale winning ad sets incrementally by 20 percent every 48 to 72 hours while introducing new creative variations to prevent ad fatigue and maintain performance. Begin building lookalike audiences from converted leads and introduce the Proof angle ad to add social credibility to the funnel.

Weeks 9-12
Results

With optimized campaigns running at full efficiency, the system should be generating a consistent flow of qualified leads that are being converted through the sales process into paying clients. The primary target is to close 20 new clients by the end of week 12 by maintaining a strong follow-up cadence, a refined qualification process, and a tested, high-performing ad ecosystem.
