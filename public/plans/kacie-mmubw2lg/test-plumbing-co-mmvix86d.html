90-Day Roadmap
Week 1
Foundation

Set up Facebook Business Manager, Google Ads account, conversion tracking, and a high-converting landing page or lead form optimized for plumbing service inquiries. Define targeting parameters, install the Facebook Pixel on the website, and create the initial ad creative for all four angles before any spend is deployed.

Week 2
Launch

Go live on both Facebook and Google at the full $500/day budget, splitting spend between Pain/Empathy and Offer/Value ad angles to identify which message resonates most with the local audience. Monitor click-through rates, cost per lead, and lead quality daily, making micro-adjustments to bids and audiences to ensure efficient early performance.

Weeks 3-4
Optimize

Analyze data from the first two weeks to identify the top-performing ad angle, audience segment, and platform, then shift budget toward what is driving the lowest cost per qualified lead. Pause underperforming ad sets, test new headlines and primary text variations on winning angles, and refine the lead qualification follow-up process to improve booking rates.

Weeks 5-8
Scale

Scale the winning campaigns by expanding lookalike audiences, layering in retargeting ads for warm website visitors, and testing Google Local Services Ads alongside Search campaigns to capture high-intent homeowners actively searching for plumbers. Introduce the Proof/Results ad angle with any testimonials or job photos collected in the first month to build social credibility and lower cost per lead further.

Weeks 9-12
Results

By this phase, the campaign infrastructure is fully optimized and generating a consistent, predictable flow of qualified plumbing leads designed to hit the 90-day goal of Get 20 new customers for Test Plumbing Co. Conduct a full performance review with John Smith, document what is working, build a referral and review generation system to compound results beyond paid advertising.
