90-Day Roadmap
Foundation

Set up the Facebook Business Manager, Instagram ad account, Meta Pixel, and lead form or landing page to ensure full tracking is in place before spending. Create three to five ad creatives across the Pain, Value, and Proof angles with clear copy tailored to the ajnfas brand.

Launch

Launch cold audience campaigns on Facebook and Instagram at $1,000 per day split across multiple ad sets to begin gathering performance data at scale. Monitor frequency, CPM, CTR, and lead cost daily to identify early signals of what is resonating.

Optimize

Pause underperforming ad sets and double budget into the creatives and audiences delivering the lowest cost per lead and highest engagement. A/B test new headlines and primary text variations based on early data to continuously improve performance.

Scale

Scale winning campaigns horizontally by expanding into lookalike audiences and new interest stacks while maintaining cost efficiency. Introduce retargeting campaigns for warm audiences who engaged but did not convert, using urgency-driven creative.

Results

Consolidate learnings from eight weeks of live data into a refined media buying strategy that maximizes lead volume within the $1,000 per day budget.
