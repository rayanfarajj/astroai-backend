90-Day Roadmap
Week 1
Foundation

Set up the Facebook Business Manager, ad account, pixel, and lead form or landing page destination to ensure all tracking and lead capture infrastructure is fully operational. Develop three to five creative assets across the Pain, Offer, and Proof angles to prepare for a structured launch.

Week 2
Launch

Go live with the top-performing ad angles at $222/day split across two to three ad sets targeting cold audiences with broad demographic and interest-based parameters. Monitor early performance data closely and pause any ad sets with below-average click-through rates or zero leads within the first 72 hours.

Weeks 3-4
Optimize

Analyze cost-per-lead, CTR, and engagement data to identify the top one or two performing creatives and audiences, then consolidate budget behind those winners. Introduce a retargeting campaign for warm audiences who engaged with the awareness ads but did not convert during the initial launch window.

Weeks 5-8
Scale

Gradually scale the daily budget on proven ad sets by 15 to 20 percent every five to seven days to maintain algorithmic stability while increasing lead volume. Launch lookalike audiences built from lead form submissions and video viewers to expand reach to high-probability new prospects.

Weeks 9-12
Results

Evaluate the full 90-day campaign performance against the primary goal of generating consistent, qualified leads for ASD at a sustainable cost-per-lead. Produce a full performance report with recommendations for creative refresh, audience expansion, and budget reallocation to maintain lead flow beyond the initial campaign period.
