90-Day Roadmap
Foundation

Set up the complete Facebook Business Manager and Instagram ad infrastructure including pixel installation, conversion tracking, custom audiences, and campaign architecture tailored to lead generation. Build and finalize all creative assets, ad copy variations, landing pages or lead forms, and CRM or lead notification systems to prepare for a strong launch.

Launch

Activate all campaigns across Facebook and Instagram at the $1,000 per day budget, deploying Pain, Offer, and Proof ad angles simultaneously to test which resonates most with the target audience. Monitor delivery, early engagement signals, click-through rates, and lead form submissions daily to catch any early issues and ensure healthy campaign performance out of the gate.

Optimize

Analyze performance data across all ad angles, audiences, and creatives to identify the top-performing combinations driving the lowest cost per lead and highest quality prospects. Pause underperformers, reallocate budget toward winning ads, refine audience targeting based on real data, and begin A/B testing new headline and copy variations to push performance further.

Scale

Scale the highest-performing campaigns with controlled budget increases of 20–30% every few days to grow lead volume without destabilizing delivery or spiking costs. Introduce lookalike audiences built from engaged leads and website visitors, and expand creative variety with new angles, video content, and testimonial-based ads to maintain momentum at scale.

Results

By this stage, the system should be delivering a consistent, scalable flow of qualified leads through Facebook and Instagram that directly supports Rayan Faraj's 90-day goal of generating leads and growing revenue. Conduct a full performance review, document winning strategies, and build a long-term scaling roadmap that continues compounding results beyond the initial 90-day sprint.
