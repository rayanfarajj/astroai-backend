90-Day Roadmap
Foundation

Set up the Facebook Business Manager, ad account, pixel, and Instagram professional profile ensuring all tracking and conversion events are firing correctly on the website or landing page. Build the creative assets, write ad copy for all four angles, and construct the campaign architecture including campaign objectives, ad sets, and audience segments across Facebook and Instagram.

Launch

Launch the initial awareness and lead generation campaigns at $1,000 per day split across Pain, Offer, and Proof ad angles targeting cold audiences on Facebook and Instagram simultaneously. Monitor delivery, frequency, click-through rates, and cost per lead daily to identify early signals of what creative and audience combinations are gaining traction.

Optimize

Analyze performance data across all active ad sets and pause underperforming creatives while allocating more budget toward the top-performing angles, audiences, and placements. A/B test new headline and primary text variations based on early data insights to continuously improve cost per lead and lead quality across both platforms.

Scale

Scale winning ad sets by increasing budgets incrementally by 20-30 percent every three to four days to maintain algorithm stability while expanding reach to broader and lookalike audiences. Introduce retargeting campaigns for all website visitors and social media engagers using the warm audience follow-up creative to maximize conversion rates from already-interested prospects.

Results

Consolidate the highest-performing campaigns into a streamlined lead generation system that consistently delivers qualified leads at a predictable cost per lead in support of the 90-day goal to generate leads and grow revenue. Produce a full performance report with CPL benchmarks, revenue attribution, and a scaling roadmap for months four through six to continue compounding growth.
