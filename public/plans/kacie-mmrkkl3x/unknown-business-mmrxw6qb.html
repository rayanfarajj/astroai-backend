90-Day Roadmap
Foundation

Set up Facebook Business Manager, install and verify the Meta Pixel on all landing pages, and create custom conversion events to track lead form submissions and strategy call bookings. Build out the core campaign structure across Facebook and Instagram including cold audience campaigns, retargeting audiences, and a high-converting lead capture asset such as a free audit offer or strategy session landing page.

Launch

Launch all ad campaigns at the full $1,000 per day budget, split across cold traffic campaigns using Pain/Empathy and Offer/Value ad angles on both Facebook and Instagram placements. Begin collecting performance data across all ad sets, monitoring cost per click, click-through rate, cost per lead, and lead quality on a daily basis to identify early signals.

Optimize

Analyze all campaign data to identify the top-performing ad angles, audiences, and placements, then pause underperformers and reallocate budget to winning combinations. A/B test new ad creative variations including different headlines, primary text, and visuals to continuously improve click-through rates and lower the cost per qualified lead.

Scale

Scale winning ad sets by gradually increasing budgets by 20–30% every 3–5 days to maximize lead volume without disrupting algorithm performance and ad delivery. Expand into lookalike audiences built from converted leads and engaged users, and layer in the Proof/Results ad angle to capture prospects who need social validation before taking action.

Results

By the end of 90 days, campaigns should be fully optimized with a consistent, predictable flow of qualified leads coming in daily from Facebook and Instagram at the lowest sustainable cost per lead. Compile a full performance report documenting lead volume, cost per lead, conversion rates, and revenue attributed to campaigns, and build a scaling roadmap for the next 90-day growth phase to continue achieving the core goal of generating leads and growing revenue.
