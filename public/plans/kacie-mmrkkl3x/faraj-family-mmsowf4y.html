90-Day Roadmap
Foundation

Set up Meta Business Manager with properly structured Facebook and Instagram campaigns, install the Meta Pixel on the landing page, and configure Google Ads with call-only and search campaigns targeting storm damage and roof replacement keywords in the 40-mile San Antonio radius. Build the landing page with a single CTA driving phone calls, and configure call tracking so every lead source is measured from day one.

Launch

Go live on Meta with three ad sets targeting the core avatar using the Pain/Empathy and Offer/Value angles at $20/day split across Facebook and Instagram placements. Simultaneously launch Google Search Ads targeting high-intent queries like 'free roof inspection San Antonio' and 'storm damage roof repair near me' to capture homeowners actively searching right now.

Optimize

Review CTR, cost-per-lead, and call volume data to identify which ad angles and audiences are driving the most qualified inspection requests at the lowest cost. Pause underperforming ad sets, double down on winning creatives — especially before/after video formats which have historically driven strong reach — and begin A/B testing new headlines and hooks.

Scale

Increase daily budget on winning Meta and Google campaigns by 20–30% every 5–7 days as long as CPL stays within target, and introduce the Proof/Results video ad angle to build trust with mid-funnel audiences. Launch retargeting campaigns on Meta targeting website visitors and video viewers who haven't yet called, using the warm audience follow-up creative.

Results

By week 12, all systems should be optimized to consistently generate 25 or more qualified inspection requests per month across Meta and Google at a sustainable cost per lead. Track closed jobs against the $8,500 average target, report on monthly ROI, and prepare a scaling plan to increase budget and expand lookalike audiences heading into peak storm season.
