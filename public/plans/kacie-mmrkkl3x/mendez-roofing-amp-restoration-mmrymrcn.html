90-Day Roadmap
Foundation

Set up the Facebook Business Manager, Instagram ad account, and TikTok Ads Manager with proper pixel tracking, conversion events, and a dedicated landing page or Meta Lead Form optimized for inspection bookings. Create the core creative assets — at minimum two video ads and two image ads using the Pain and Offer angles — and build out all audience segments including custom and lookalike audiences.

Launch

Launch campaigns across Meta and TikTok at a combined $75 per day budget, split approximately $50 on Meta and $25 on TikTok, running the Pain and Offer angles simultaneously to test which resonates best with San Antonio homeowners. Monitor frequency, click-through rate, and cost per lead daily to catch any early issues with creative fatigue or targeting misalignment before they drain budget.

Optimize

Analyze which ad angles, headlines, and creatives are producing the lowest cost per inspection request and pause underperformers while increasing spend on winners by 20 percent every 3 days. Begin layering in the Proof angle with any early testimonials or job photos collected, and launch the retargeting campaign to re-engage website visitors and video viewers who did not convert.

Scale

Scale the top-performing campaigns by gradually increasing daily budget on Meta and TikTok while expanding geographic targeting to capture surrounding cities like New Braunfels, Boerne, and Seguin within the 40-mile radius. Introduce new creative variations every two weeks to prevent ad fatigue and test video testimonials or walkthrough inspection videos on TikTok to build organic-style trust at paid scale.

Results

By this phase the system should be consistently delivering 25 or more qualified inspection requests per month at a cost per lead under $60, with a close rate targeting 10 jobs per month at an $8,500 average job value. Use this period to build out a customer referral loop and collect video testimonials that feed back into the ad system, creating a compounding trust engine that reduces cost per lead over time.
