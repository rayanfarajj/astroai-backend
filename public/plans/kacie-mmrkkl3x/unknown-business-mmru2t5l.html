90-Day Roadmap
Foundation

Audit and set up the Facebook Business Manager, Ads Manager, and Meta Pixel. Build out the lead gen funnel including landing page or native lead form, creative assets, and audience segments. Define campaign structure across awareness, consideration, and conversion objectives and install all tracking and conversion events.

Launch

Launch initial test campaigns across Empathy and Offer ad angles targeting cold audiences of business owners and marketing decision-makers on Facebook and Instagram. Run A/B tests on headlines, primary text, and creatives with a controlled daily budget to identify early winners.

Optimize

Analyze performance data including CTR, CPL, lead quality, and form completion rates. Kill underperforming ad sets and reallocate budget to top performers. Refine audience targeting based on demographic and interest data, and begin A/B testing landing page or lead form variations to improve conversion rates.

Scale

Scale winning ad sets by incrementally increasing budgets by 20-30 percent every 3-5 days to avoid algorithmic disruption. Introduce Proof and Retargeting campaign layers to capture warm audiences and re-engage non-converting leads. Expand creative library with new angles and formats including video and Reels.

Goal

Hit the primary 90-day goal of generating a consistent, scalable flow of qualified leads and measurable revenue growth. Deliver a full performance report with CPL benchmarks, pipeline value generated, and a forward-looking strategy for continued scaling beyond the initial campaign period.
