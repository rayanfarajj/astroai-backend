90-Day Roadmap
Week 1
Foundation

Set up the Facebook and Instagram ad accounts, install tracking pixels, and create all creative assets including ad copy, visuals, and landing page elements. Establish baseline audience segments and confirm campaign structure is airtight before any budget is deployed.

Week 2
Launch

Deploy the initial awareness and lead generation campaigns at $222/day across Facebook and Instagram, focusing on broad audience testing with multiple ad angles. Monitor early performance data daily to identify which creatives and audiences are generating the strongest engagement and lowest cost per click.

Weeks 3-4
Optimize

Pause underperforming ad sets and reallocate budget toward the top-performing angles, audiences, and placements identified in Week 2. Begin A/B testing headlines, primary text variations, and call-to-action buttons to squeeze more efficiency out of the daily spend.

Weeks 5-8
Scale

Scale winning campaigns horizontally by introducing new audience segments and vertically by increasing budgets on proven ad sets with controlled increments. Layer in retargeting campaigns for warm audiences who engaged but did not convert, creating a full-funnel approach.

Weeks 9-12
Results

Measure all campaign results against the daesds goal, consolidating insights into a performance report that identifies what drove the highest return. Use this data to refine the strategy for the next quarter and lock in repeatable systems that continue delivering results for xcvc.
