90-Day Roadmap
Foundation

Set up Facebook Business Manager, ad accounts, Meta Pixel, and conversion tracking across Facebook and Instagram to ensure every dollar is measurable. Define campaign structure, build creative assets, write ad copy across all four angles, and configure lead capture forms or landing pages.

Launch

Launch all awareness and lead generation campaigns across Facebook and Instagram at the full $1,000 per day budget with multiple ad sets testing different audiences and creatives simultaneously. Monitor delivery, relevance scores, and early engagement data daily to catch any technical issues immediately.

Optimize

Analyze performance data across all ad sets and creative versions, pausing underperformers and reallocating budget toward the angles and audiences generating the lowest cost per lead. A/B test new headlines, primary text variations, and audience segments informed by real campaign data.

Scale

Scale winning ad sets by increasing budgets incrementally by 20–30% every 3–5 days to avoid disrupting algorithm learning while expanding reach to new lookalike and interest-based audiences. Introduce retargeting campaigns to re-engage warm audiences who interacted but did not convert.

Results

By this phase, the campaign system should be delivering a consistent, predictable volume of qualified leads each month aligned with the 90-day goal of generating leads at scale. Review full-funnel performance, calculate cost per lead benchmarks, and build a scaling roadmap for the next quarter based on proven winners.
