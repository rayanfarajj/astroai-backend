90-Day Roadmap
Week 1-2
Foundation & Setup

Install Meta Pixel and Conversions API on website. Set up offline conversion tracking for phone calls and booked appointments. Build all custom audiences (website visitors, engagers, email lists) and generate lookalike audiences. Create and organize ad account campaign structure. Design all ad creatives across 5 angles (Empathy, Pain Points, Proof, Curiosity, Retargeting). Set up lead forms with qualifying questions. Configure CRM integration for instant lead notifications. Establish baseline KPIs and reporting dashboards.

Week 3-4
Launch & Test

Launch Empathy, Pain Points, and Curiosity ad angles as cold traffic campaigns using broad, interest-based, and lookalike audiences. Allocate approximately $800/mo to cold prospecting and $300/mo to retargeting. Run 3-4 ad variations per angle for A/B testing. Activate retargeting campaign for website visitors and engagers with the $50 off offer. Monitor daily for delivery issues, disapprovals, or audience overlap. Begin lead follow-up within 5 minutes of form submission using the qualification script.

Week 5-8
Optimize & Refine

Analyze performance data and pause underperforming ad variations (CTR below 1%, CPL above $40). Shift budget toward top 2-3 winning ads and audiences. Test new creative variations of winning angles. Introduce Proof angle ads with testimonial-based content. Optimize lead form questions to improve lead quality. A/B test landing pages if website traffic campaigns are active. Refine retargeting sequences — add testimonial retargeting ads. Target CPL of $15-30 and aim for 4-6 booked appointments per week. Adjust frequency caps on retargeting to prevent ad fatigue.

Week 9-12
Scale & Expand

Scale winning campaigns by increasing daily budgets 20-30% every 3-4 days on top performers. Expand lookalike audiences to 2-3% for broader reach. Introduce seasonal or promotional offers to boost conversion rates. Launch Instagram Reels and Stories placements with short-form video content. Test Advantage+ campaign budget optimization for automated scaling. Implement referral incentives for converted clients to generate organic leads. Compile monthly performance report with CPL, conversion rate, ROAS, and client acquisition metrics. Project forward for month 4+ strategy based on data insights.
