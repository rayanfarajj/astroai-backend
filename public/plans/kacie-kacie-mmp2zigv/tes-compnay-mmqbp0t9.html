90-Day Roadmap
Week 1-2
Foundation & Setup

Set up Google Ads account structure, install conversion tracking via Google Tag Manager, configure Google Analytics 4 goals for appointment bookings and calls, build dedicated landing pages for each ad angle, conduct comprehensive keyword research targeting high-intent dental search terms, set up call tracking, create Google Business Profile optimization, and establish baseline KPI dashboards.

Week 3-4
Campaign Launch

Launch search campaigns across all five ad angles with tightly themed ad groups, activate responsive search ads with multiple headline and description variations, begin with manual CPC bidding to gather initial data, deploy location-targeted campaigns within the service area, launch call-only ads for mobile users, initiate Google Business Profile ads, and begin A/B testing landing page variants for each angle.

Week 5-8
Optimize & Refine

Analyze initial performance data across all ad angles and pause underperformers, add negative keywords to eliminate wasted spend, transition top-performing campaigns to Target CPA or Maximize Conversions bidding, optimize ad copy based on CTR and conversion rate data, refine audience targeting based on demographic performance insights, improve landing page conversion rates through iterative testing, and activate retargeting campaigns for website visitors who did not convert.

Week 9-12
Scale & Expand

Double down on the top 2-3 performing ad angles and keyword themes, expand keyword coverage to capture broader dental service queries, increase budget allocation to highest-ROAS campaigns, scale retargeting with sequential messaging for warm leads, introduce Display remarketing banners for additional touchpoints, test Performance Max campaigns for cross-channel reach, build lookalike audiences from converted patients, and prepare monthly reporting with insights for ongoing optimization beyond Week 12.
