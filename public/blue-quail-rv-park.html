07 / Competitor Positioning
5 Ways to Stand Above the Competition
01
Lead with Free Towing
Always mention the free towing service first in all marketing materials and conversations. This unique offer immediately differentiates you from every competitor and removes the biggest barrier to switching parks.
02
Emphasize Value, Not Just Price
Position the $450 monthly rate as exceptional value when combined with free towing, cleanliness, and management quality. Help prospects calculate their total cost savings versus alternatives including storage fees and moving costs.
03
Highlight Community Over Amenities
Focus on the peaceful, well-managed community aspect rather than competing on amenities you may not have. RV owners seeking permanent housing value safety, cleanliness, and good neighbors over fancy facilities.
04
Use Urgency Without Pressure
Create natural urgency by mentioning limited available sites and the current free towing promotion. Frame it as helping them secure their spot rather than pushing them to decide quickly.
05
Address Moving Anxiety Head-On
Acknowledge that moving an RV is stressful and expensive, then position your free towing service as the solution that makes everything easy. Use phrases like 'hassle-free' and 'we handle everything' to reinforce this benefit.
