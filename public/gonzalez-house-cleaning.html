08 / Lead Qualification Script
Ready-to-Use Call Script
Opening
Hi! Thanks for requesting a quote with Gonzalez House Cleaning. I'm Maria, and I'd love to learn about your home and cleaning needs to make sure we're a perfect fit.
Qualifying Questions
Q1
How many square feet is your home?
Why: Determines if they meet minimum 1500 sq ft requirement and scope of work
Q2
Are you looking for weekly or biweekly recurring cleaning?
Why: Confirms they want ongoing service, not one-time deep clean
Q3
What's most important to you in a cleaning service?
Why: Reveals if they value reliability, consistency, and quality
Q4
Have you used a cleaning service before, and what was your experience?
Why: Identifies pain points and positions our same-cleaner guarantee
Q5
Are you a homeowner, and do you own the property?
Why: Disqualifies renters without landlord approval
Transition to Close
Perfect! Based on what you've shared, I think we could be a great fit. Here's what sets us apart: you'll get the same trusted cleaner every visit, we bring all supplies, and we guarantee your satisfaction with a free re-clean within 24 hours if needed.
Objection Handling
❌ "I want to provide my own cleaning supplies"
✅ We appreciate that, but our cleaning method and products are proven to work best together. Plus, you save money not buying supplies, and we handle everything—it's truly worry-free.
❌ "I'm worried about trusting someone in my home"
✅ That's exactly why we commit to sending the same cleaner every visit. You build a relationship with one person who knows your home. All our staff are fully background-checked and insured.
❌ "How much does it cost?"
✅ It depends on your home size and frequency, but most of our recurring weekly customers pay around $200-300/week. We offer a free estimate, and you can see the exact cost before committing.
