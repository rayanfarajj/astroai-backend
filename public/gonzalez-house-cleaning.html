08 / Lead Qualification Script
Ready-to-Use Call Script
Opening
Thanks for reaching out! I'm excited to learn about your home and cleaning needs. I want to make sure Gonzalez House Cleaning is the perfect fit for you.
Qualifying Questions
Q1
What's your home's square footage and how many bedrooms/bathrooms?
Why: Qualify that they have 1500+ sq ft; larger homes are ideal for recurring service
Q2
Are you currently having your home professionally cleaned, or would this be your first time?
Why: Understand their cleaning history and expectations
Q3
Would you prefer weekly or biweekly cleaning, and why?
Why: Confirm they want recurring service (our core offering), not one-time deep cleans
Q4
What's most important to you in a cleaning service—reliability, consistency, value, or something else?
Why: Understand their priorities; align with our same-cleaner guarantee and quality promise
Q5
Are you the homeowner, or would you need to get approval from a landlord or property manager?
Why: Disqualify renters without landlord approval; qualify homeowners only
Transition to Close
Based on what you've shared, Gonzalez House Cleaning sounds like a great match. Here's what makes us different: same trusted cleaner every visit, we bring all supplies, fully insured, and we guarantee satisfaction with a 24-hour re-clean policy.
Objection Handling
❌ "I want to supply my own cleaning products."
✅ I understand, but our EPA-approved products are eco-friendly, safe, and proven. Plus, providing supplies ensures consistent results and saves you the cost and hassle.
❌ "I'm worried about a stranger in my home."
✅ That's completely valid. All our cleaners are background-checked, insured, and you get the same person every visit so you build trust quickly. Many clients say this is their favorite benefit.
❌ "Your price seems high compared to other services."
✅ You're paying for consistency—same trusted cleaner, no surprises, guaranteed quality, and our 24-hour guarantee. Many clients save money long-term because they don't need reschedules or extra cleanings.

5 Ways to Stand Above the Competition
01

Own the 'Same Cleaner Every Visit' Angle

This is your biggest differentiator. Emphasize how consistency builds trust, familiarity, and better cleaning results. Make it the hero of your messaging.

Your 24-hour re-clean guarantee is powerful proof of confidence. Highlight it early and often—it removes risk and builds trust immediately.

Position yourself as the solution to decision fatigue and time pressure. Your service gives them back hours every week—quantify and celebrate that benefit.

Busy professionals buy peace of mind, not just cleaning. Frame your service as freedom—from stress, from decision-making, from cleaning day chaos.
