08 / Lead Qualification Script
Ready-to-Use Call Script
Opening
Hi! Thanks for reaching out to Gonzalez House Cleaning. I want to make sure we're a perfect fit for your needs. Mind if I ask you a few quick questions?
Qualifying Questions
Q1
How large is your home, and how many bathrooms does it have?
Why: Confirms if property meets 1500+ sq ft minimum and helps calculate accurate pricing
Q2
Are you looking for weekly, biweekly, or flexible recurring cleaning?
Why: Ensures they want recurring service, not one-time deep clean; confirms fit
Q3
What's been your biggest frustration with keeping your home clean?
Why: Identifies pain point and allows us to position ourselves as solution
Q4
How important is having the same cleaner visit your home each time?
Why: Validates our key differentiator and ensures they value consistency
Q5
When would you ideally like to schedule your first cleaning?
Why: Gauges urgency and commitment level; moves toward closing
Transition to Close
Perfect! Based on what you've shared, I think we'd be a great fit. Let me walk you through how we work and what to expect from our first cleaning.
Objection Handling
❌ "We've used other cleaners before and they were unreliable."
✅ I hear you—that's the #1 complaint we solve for. You get the same trusted person every visit, and we guarantee your satisfaction or we re-clean free within 24 hours. No excuses.
❌ "How much does it cost?"
✅ Pricing depends on home size and frequency, but most customers invest $2-3 per sq ft per month for weekly service. Given the time you'll save and peace of mind, most families say it's one of their best decisions. Want me to give you a custom quote?
❌ "I'm not sure if I can commit to recurring service right now."
✅ No pressure at all. Why don't we start with just one cleaning and see if we're the right fit? If you love it, we transition to recurring. If not, there's zero obligation. How does that sound?
09 / Competitor Positioning
5 Ways to Stand Above the Competition
01

Lead with Consistency

Your #1 message is 'same cleaner every visit.' People want to know and trust the person in their home. This is your biggest differentiator.

02

Emphasize the Guarantee

The 24-hour re-clean guarantee removes risk and shows confidence. Lead with this in all copy and conversations—it's a powerful closer.

03

Focus on Time Recovery

Don't just sell cleaning; sell the hours back to their life. 'You get 5 hours back every month to spend with family, hobbies, or rest.'

04

Highlight Full Insurance & Background Checks

Busy professionals with nice homes worry about trust. Emphasize you're fully insured and all staff are background-checked—this is peace of mind.

05

Own the Supplies Angle

'We bring everything' removes one more task. This is convenience that prospects don't expect from a cleaner—use it as a surprise delight.
